**Selecting pages that require changes**

To select pages, the `transition_status` and `transition_comment` fields must be added to the yaml header as follows:

```markdown
---
layout: default
title: 10.06 Resources
parent: 10 Lab Processes
grand_parent: Lab Management
nav_order: 7
transition_status: "toupdate"
transition_comment: "Update keys, lab access, printing/scanning, design, archive"
---

# 10.06 Resources

contents ....
```

Once the transition is implemented, the `transition_status` can be changed to `updated`.
